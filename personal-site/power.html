Power Ammo Weapons

Power Weapons are the highest damage dealing weapons there are other than your super abilities, but with the right weapons and damage outputs you can deal over a million damage in no time. The types of Power weapons are, Linear Fusions, Fusions, Rocket Launchers, Heavy Granade Launcher, Glaives, Bows. here are a couple of the best weapons there are!

The Apex Predator

The Apex Predator is the highest damage rocket launcher there is. It is obtained from the Last Wish Raid in The Dreaming City the weapon in of it self is a master of craftsmanship and built to dish out damage. The best perks on this weapon are Bait and Switch with Reconstruction. Bait and Switch is a powerful perk to have it activiates when dealing damage with all your weapons and for 10 sections you have a 35% damage increase. Pair it with reconstruction, and you have a rocket that reloads every 4 sectonds and if used properly you can have 2 rockets everytime you switch to it. Granting you the highest damage per second possible.

Cataclysmic Linear

The Cataclysmic Linear Fusion is a precision monster able to out damage many linears. This weapon is also obtained from the Vow of Deciple Raid on the Throne World. The rolls that are best for this weapon are Fourth Time's the Charm and Bait and Switch. Fourth Time's the Charm works by rapidly landing percision hits will refund rounds into the magazine. It syncronizes with Bait and Switch by causeing you to not waste time with those 10 seconds, and being able to put out as much damage as possible before the time runs out.
