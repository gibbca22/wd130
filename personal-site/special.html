Special Ammo Weapons

Special weapons specialize in taking down champions and majors down fast. But they can also take down a room of enemies out in one shot, there are a few different types of special weapons. the types are Fusion Rifles, Grenade Launchers, Linear Fusion Rifles, Sidearms, and Glaives. Each has their own benefit but were going to bring the favorites out.

The Eremite

The Eremite ornaate looking weapon and is a fusion rifle achieved from The Season of the Witch this weapon can roll with a few different perks. but the main perks are Envious Assassin and Controlled Burst. These two perks go perfect together, Envious Assassin when getting kills with your primary it transfers one round from reserves to your magazine, possibly loading all your reserves. Combine it with Controlled Burst you got yourself a damage machine. Controlled Burst provides a damage and charge time buff, when landing all your shots on target. when you have 20 rounds and the damage buff stacks you have yourself one of the highest damage fusion rifles.

Forbearance

The Forbearance is a Wave Frame Grenade Launcher and is obtained from the Vow of the Deciple Raid on The Throne World. This is a beloved weapon by everyone in an ad-clear senario. It is able to take out hoards of minor enemies in one shot and continue ot do for a wile. The main perks to use on this weapon are Ambistous Assassin and Chain Reaction. Ambitious Assassin works a little differently than Envious Assassin but they are very similar in liking but the difference between the two is that Ambitious Assassin reloads to a 150% of the magazine rounding up which will only give you 2 shots in this grenade launcher. But what makes this better is Chain Reaction, this perk on a final blow of an enemy creates an elemental explosion damaging everything aroundit causing everything in its path to erupt.
